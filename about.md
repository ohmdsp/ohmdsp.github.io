My interests span the column space of a rank N matrix between engineering and physics. I try to keep that matrix symmetric so that the sum of eigenvalues equal the trace. This could explain why I like to mangle adverbs. Who knows. <br>

I grew up when MTV played heavy metal late Saturday nights. That likely has nothing to do with anything, but I did go on to graduate with a Bachelor's degree in Physics with an emphasis in lasers and optics, then completed a Master's and PhD in Electrical Engineering with a obsessive focus on statistical signal processing. Regardless of all the playtime with lasers, I can still see out of both my eyes and I am currently the co-founder of a technology company that develops innovative capabilities for extracting information from sensor data. How fun is that! <br>

I hold firm to the rule that when software is involved, misery is conserved. However, the equation is too messy to fit on a T-shirt. <br> 

I also enjoy creating [music](https://soundcloud.com/davidohm) and occasionally spending time in the mountains and on the rivers.
